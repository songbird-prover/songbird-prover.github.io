The Songbird prover

An inductive theorem prover for Separation Logic entailments

Welcome to our home page!

Songbird is an automated theorem prover for Separation Logic entailments. At its core, Songbird employs mathematical induction to prove entailments involving user-defined inductive heap predicates.

In addition, Songbird is also equipped with powerful proof techniques to assist in proving the entailments. These include a mutual induction proof technique to speculate lemmas from entailments derived in proof trees or an automatic lemma synthesis to derive neccessary lemmas from scratch. These techniques are described in detail in our sub-projects:

  • Songbird + Mutual Induction
  • Songbird + Lemma Synthesis
